Creative greeting card genarator

The web app generates personalized and creative poem/prosa/haiku greeting cards automatically when given user's information. The recipients can be friends, family, loved ones, or colleagues depending on users' needs. As the Chinese New Year comes soon, the user can also generate personalized and creative Chinese New Year's greeting cards upon their needs. This program also includes a feedback platform that provides a user with an option to add their own datasets if they are not satisfied with the outputs.
